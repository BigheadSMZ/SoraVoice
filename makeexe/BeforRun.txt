put this folder 'makeexe' in the root of the project 'SoraVoice'

put FC_main.exe,SC_main.exe,3RD_main.exe in makeexe\main\
put nasm.exe(an 80x86 assembler, please use a stable version) in makeexe\nasm\
compile Importer_EXE and put it in makeexe\ (this folder)

run 'run.bat'

then you will get fc_voice.exe, sc_voice.exe, 3rd_voice.exe under makeexe\new\
